About me

Hello, I'm Kammeron Nhieu currently studying at the Coding Dojo in San Jose. I've always wanted to have the ability to construct the ideas I have inside in my head. Thanks to the Coding Dojo I have learned how to develop full-stack web/mobile applications in Python, JavaScript, and Swift.
Before the Coding Dojo, by day I was a medical assistant at Action Urgent Care and by night I was asleep. I enjoyed the work I did, however I found that the medical field is not what I wanted to continue pursuing. So in the summer of 2018 I took a leap of faith and decided to delve deeping into the field of software development. I am currently in the last few weeks of school and I am excited to pursue this newfound passion even further.
